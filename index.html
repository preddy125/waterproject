About Me

Hi, I'm Nishka!

I'm a high school student passionate about coding, learning, and building things that matter. This website is my digital space where I document my projects, share learning resources, and connect with others on similar journeys.

My goal is to create value through code, whether that's building useful tools, writing helpful tutorials, or curating resources that help others learn. I'm always working on new projects and learning new technologies.

Current Focus: HOA Water Management, Summer Fair for Charity, and building practical tools that solve real problems.
